Projects

Below are a few of my personal projects! A few of them are finished but some of them are still a work in progress.

Webify is a local business firm established to support and help local organizations, businesses, and restaurants during the COVID-19 pandemic. We swiftly provide and develop modern, clean websites to struggling businesses in need free of all charges. Visit us at webifybayarea.com to learn more.
LaTex Converter is able to convert handwritten equations and formulas into LaTex syntax. Users are able to handwrite a formula on our website template. We then splice and seperate each character from the input formula, then use a CNN to predict each individual character. The predicted equation then is then turned into LateX form. Click here for source code and to learn more.
